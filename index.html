02 — The Story

Some recipes are too precious to write down.

Bellucci's started in 1924 with a wood counter, a hand-written notebook of family recipes, and the simple idea that food made with care tastes different. A century later, very little has changed.

We still bake before sunrise. We still slice prosciutto by hand. And we still answer the phone — because catering an event isn't a transaction, it's an invitation into our family.

Antonio Bellucci 4th Generation Owner
